How I became Director of a Tech Company at the age of 21

The first time I ever made money was from selling CD's to my classmates in school which had the files to download a popular game called GTA Vice City, Now that I look back at this event, I realize that subconsciously I've always known my calling: 1) I was good at technology and 2) I had an entrepreneurial spirit which would take me places in life!

Down the years, I've taken every opportunity that's come my way to exhibit my Leadership skills, Be it the Professional Sport that I played for 15 years or the numerous Group projects that I've been part of. So, During my Bachelors, when I started working for a Cloud Computing Startup as an Intern at the age of 19, I knew I had a great opportunity in front of me and I wanted to make the most of it. So, I put my dancing shoes and my thinking hat on and I was away!
